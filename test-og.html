How to Check:

  1. Open your app in the browser
  2. Open DevTools (F12)
  3. Go to Console tab
  4. Paste and run this code:

Quick Checklist:

  • ✅ og:title is present
  • ✅ og:description is present
  • ✅ og:image is present and is an absolute URL (starts with http:// or https://)
  • ✅ og:url is present
  • ✅ Image URL is accessible (you can open it directly in browser)
  • ✅ Image dimensions are at least 1200x630px (recommended)

Important Notes:

  • Image must be in the public folder: Place social-media-preview.png in the public folder at the root of your project
  • Image must be accessible: The URL should work when you open it directly in a browser
  • Use absolute URLs: Social media crawlers need full URLs, not relative paths
  • Test with tunnel: For full testing, use ngrok or similar to expose your localhost to social media validators
